Introducing e-Lite 2016

Exun Clan's annual induction test for Clasess 6th to 11th is here, and we're looking for individuals with fresh talent and great enthusiasm. Do you think you fit in?

Only for Students studying in Delhi Public School, R.K. Puram.

Details



The Onsite Events will be held after school hours on 20 July 2016 from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM. No student shall be allowed to stay back without the submission of the consent slip to Mr. Mukesh Kumar, HOD, Computer Science (either in school or via email).


Consent Slip for Stayback


Senior & Junior Quiz

If you have a brain that relentlessly collects tidbits of knowledge of the tech world, ever ready to work your neural networks and conjure an answer out of the blue, then quizzing is for you!


Eligibility – Senior (9th – 11th) , Junior (6th – 8th)
Participants per team: Up to 2

Hardware

If you take apart every device you can get your hands on, and are the go-to guy whenever your friends need to buy a new phone or laptop, then power up your cores and overclock that grey matter because hardware and benchmarking is the perfect event for you!


Eligibility – 6th - 11th
Participants per team: Up to 2

Crossword

If you can draw connections between the randomest of things, and if you are a king or queen of puns with a good knowledge of the tech world, then nothing will give you greater joy than our crossword event!


Eligibility – 6th – 11th
Participants per team: Up to 2

Junior & Senior Programming

Make sure to include all your header files as you make your way through two rounds of intense logical stimulation and puzzles that will leave you bamboozled. Participants shall be required to qualify a logic-based written prelim round following which they shall face a grueling onsite final.


Eligibility – Senior (9th – 11th) , Junior (6th – 8th)
Individual Event

Group Discussion

Quick-thinking, confidence and coherence backed up by statistical data and facts ensure heated debates and discussions about topics that will be given on the spot. Make your point and accommodate other’s opinions to come out at the top!


Eligibility – 6th – 11th
Individual Event

Creative Event (Online)

Photoshop, After Effects, HTML, CSS and Creativity. Think this is your cup of tea? Join us in the creative event. This is an online event. Since this is an open event, there is no sign up required for this. The prompt is available at http://exun.co/prompt. Please follow all instructions in that document.


Eligibility – 6th - 11th
